bearCode Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to bearCode AI Assistant. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you use our AI assistant and tells you about your privacy rights.

bearCode is an open-source AI chat assistant project. This privacy policy applies to your use of the application and describes how we collect, use, and handle your information.

Data We Collect

When you use bearCode AI Assistant, we may collect the following information:

  • Chat Content: The messages you send to and receive from the AI are processed to provide you with responses.
  • Preferences: Settings such as your theme preference (light/dark/system) and AI model selection are stored locally on your device.
  • Technical Data: Basic information about your browser and device may be collected for proper functioning of the application and debugging purposes.

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the bearCode AI Assistant service
  • To process and complete your requests
  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve the application

Data Storage

bearCode primarily uses local storage in your browser to store your preferences (such as theme settings) and current chat session. This means your data stays on your device by default. Chat history may be temporarily stored on the server during your session to maintain context for the AI responses.

Third-Party Services

bearCode utilizes the g4f library to connect to AI models for generating responses. Your messages are processed by these AI models in accordance with their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party AI services if you have concerns.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except as described in this privacy policy. Your chat content may be processed by the AI models we use to generate responses.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of your personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing of your personal data
  • The right to data portability

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. We recommend checking this policy periodically for any changes.

Open Source

bearCode is an open-source project. You can view the source code and contribute to the project on GitHub. Being open-source means transparency in how your data is processed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through our Discord community.
