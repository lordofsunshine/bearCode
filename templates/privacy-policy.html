bearCode Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

bearCode is designed to keep the chat experience useful, simple, and respectful of your privacy.

Your Chats

Your recent conversations are saved so you can continue where you left off. bearCode keeps up to three recent chats for your current browser session. When a fourth chat is created, the oldest one is removed.

What Is Stored

  • Your chat messages and bearCode responses.
  • Short chat titles that help you recognize recent conversations.
  • A local browser session ID used to show your own recent chats on this device.

Clearing Chats

You can clear the current chat from the sidebar. This resets the conversation and removes its previous messages from your recent chat history.

AI Responses

bearCode can make mistakes. Please review important answers, code, commands, and factual claims before relying on them.
